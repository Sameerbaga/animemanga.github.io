Anime (Japanese: アニメ).As a type of animation.

anime is an art form that comprises of many genres found in other mediums; it is sometimes mistakenly classified as a genre itself.

Manga (Japanese: 漫画 [maŋga]). The word "manga" comes from the Japanese word 漫画.

The history of manga is said to originate from scrolls dating back to the 12th century, and it is believed they represent the basis for the right-to-left reading style. During the Edo period (1603–1867), Toba Ehon embedded the concept of manga.
